Stylist

KIMBERLEY WENAS brings a dynamic, authentic and personal approach to celebrity styling. Her fashion career kicked off while working in the PR & Marketing agency at IVY LEE in Amsterdam. Working behind the scenes of her first photoshoot, Kimberley realized styling was her calling. Constantly seeking new ways to integrate her fresh and edgy point of view with classic timelessness and sophistication. Kimberley’s clientele includes Kim Feenstra, Anna Nooshin, Jayh Jawson, Winston ‘Winne’ Bergwijn and Rolf Sanchez, to name a few. Inspired by life, her friends, family and clients, Kimberley continues to work tirelessly on expanding her business and growing her brand with an exhibition and documentary ‘Dreams Money Can’t Buy’.


Selected clients: LINDA. Meiden, Noosh Beauty, Top Notch, JFK Men, Videoland, EndemolShine Nederland
